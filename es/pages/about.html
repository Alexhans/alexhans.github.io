About

I'm Alex Hans. I've developed software professionally for approx 10 years now, but who's counting.

I've always enjoyed reading, hearing and giving advice on topics related to programming, productivity, security, methodologies, customer focus, UX, tools, proper specification and scope definition, etc.

Since I've been benefitting so much from blog posts and information shared by others, I thought I'd try and get the hang of it too so that I can contribute some of my own experiences back.

This blog will be an iterative process and I'll try to keep it simple since time is scarce (For example, it's a static website).
